Volunteers bring energy, experience and encouragement to safe and meaningful youth programs.

Become part of the team

Scouting needs volunteers with many different strengths. Some enjoy outdoor skills and program delivery, while others contribute through planning, administration, transportation, fundraising or group support.

What volunteers do

  • Support safe, inclusive and age-appropriate activities.
  • Help youth plan, participate, reflect and grow.
  • Share practical skills and positive leadership.
  • Work as part of a screened and trained volunteer team.

Screening and training

Volunteer roles require completion of the applicable registration, screening and training steps. The group will help explain the process and identify what is required for the role.

No prior Scouting experience required

You do not need to know everything before joining. A willingness to learn, collaborate and support youth is an excellent place to begin.
