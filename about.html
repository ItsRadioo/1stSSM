Welcome to 1st Sault Ste. Marie Scouts

We provide age-appropriate Scouting experiences designed to help youth become capable, confident and community-minded. Youth learn by doing, contribute to program decisions and develop practical skills through progressively more challenging adventures.

Our approach

Activities are built around participation, teamwork, outdoor experience, community involvement and reflection. Adult volunteers create a safe and supportive environment while youth take increasing ownership of their program.

What makes Scouting different

Scouting combines friendship and fun with meaningful responsibility. A simple meeting activity can become the first step toward a camp, service project, leadership role or new outdoor skill.

Our community

The group is part of the Sault Ste. Marie community. We aim to make participation welcoming and to build strong connections among youth, families, volunteers and community supporters.
