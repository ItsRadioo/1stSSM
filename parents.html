Common questions

What should my child bring to a regular meeting?

Requirements vary by activity. Your section team will identify any special clothing, equipment or forms before the meeting.

How will I receive event information?

The group may use email, ScoutsTracker or another approved communication method. Keep your contact information current and review event details carefully.

Can parents attend camps or overnight activities?

Participation requirements depend on the activity and the adult's role. Contact the section team early so screening and training requirements can be confirmed.
